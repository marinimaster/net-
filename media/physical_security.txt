What feature allows a security camera to survey a large room and pick out individual faces?
answer

Fixed positioning

Narrow focal length

Correct: Pan-Tilt-Zoom (PTZ) controls

Coaxial cabling

Ex: The correct answer is Pan-Tilt-Zoom (PTZ) controls. PTZ controls allow a camera to pan (move horizontally), tilt 
(move vertically), and zoom in on specific areas or subjects. This capability is essential for surveying large rooms 
and picking out individual faces, providing flexibility and detailed surveillance that fixed cameras cannot offer.
