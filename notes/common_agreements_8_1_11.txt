Agreements are used between a company and its employees to enforce performance and security objectives.

# Service Level Agreement (SLA)
Is a contractual agreement setting out the detailed terms under which a service is provided.

# Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)
Is the legal basis for protecting information assets. It defines which uses of sensitive data are
permitted, what storage and distribution restrictions must be enforced, and what penalties are incurred
by breaches of the agreement.

# Memorandum of Understanding (MOU)
Is a preliminary or exploratory agreement to express an intent to work together.
It is relatively informal because it is usually not a binding contract.
However, MOUs have clauses stating that parties shall respect confidentiality.
