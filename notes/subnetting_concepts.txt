Subnetting Concepts
* Subnet Mask: Defines which part of the IP address is the network and which is the host.
* CIDR (Classless Inter-Domain Routing): Uses a slash notation (e.g., /24) to represent the mask.

Benefits of Subnetting:
* Reduced network traffic (smaller broadcast domains).
* Optimized network performance.
* Simplified management.
