IMAGE 19: WIRELESS LAN STANDARDS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Wireless LAN Standards (802.11)
* 802.11a: 5 GHz, up to 54 Mbps.
* 802.11b: 2.4 GHz, up to 11 Mbps.
* 802.11g: 2.4 GHz, up to 54 Mbps.
* 802.11n: 2.4/5 GHz, up to 600 Mbps (MIMO).
