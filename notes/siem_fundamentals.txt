# Security Information and Event Management (SIEM)

Designed to integrate network and security monitoring through automated collection, aggregation and analysis 
of logged data.

Its core function is to aggregate logs from multiple sources. In addition to logs from Windows and 
Linux-based hosts. This could also include devices such as 
- Switches
- Routers
- Intrusion detection sensors
- Vulnerability scanners
- Malware scanners
- Databases
