IMAGE 10: THREE WAY HANDSHAKE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Three Way Handshake
[SYN] [SYN, ACK] [ACK]

1) Source sends sequence number to destination
   Ex: 
   Src: 10.10.0.1
   Dst: 172.120.6.1
   Syn = 15

   Destination sends an acknowledge number equal to Source's sequence number + 1.
   Ex:
   Src: 172.120.6.1
   Dst: 10.10.0.1
   Syn = 0
   Ack = 16
