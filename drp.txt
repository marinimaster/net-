# Disaster Recovery Plan (DRP)
Addresses large-scale network accidents.
1. Identify Scenarios
2. Identify Tasks
3. Train Staff

Tabletop exercises: teams discuss hypothetical scenarios.
Validation tests: focus on simulating failovers.

# Business Continuity Plan (BCP)
A collection of processes that allow a business to maintain operations in the face of an 
adverse event.

# Business Impact Analysis (BIA)
Identifies mission-essential and primary business functions and the risks that would
arise if an organization cannot fulfill them.

# IT contingency planning (ITCP)
Ensures that these functions are supported by resilient IT systems.
